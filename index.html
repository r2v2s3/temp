Are Covid-19 patients with specific blood types at higher risk of mortality?

It is a known fact that people with specific blood types have increased incidence of specific infections. Blood type to disease associations have been studied since 1900s Ref Link

Blood Type Increased incident rate associations
O plague, cholera, mumps, and tuberculosis.
A smallpox
B gonorrhea, tuberculosis, Streptococcus pneumoniae, E. coli, and salmonella infections
AB smallpox, E. coli, and salmonella infections


Similarly, there is growing evidence that Blood Type A has increased mortality rates for Covid-19 patients LA Times Article.
